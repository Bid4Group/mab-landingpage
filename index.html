Connect with the top level advisors in your field

MAB is a selective international network of expert across all industries. We connect you with top level advisors within a framework of confidentiality, trust and where you can get out a maximum of productivity and business results.

  • Start by creating your company profile. Tell us about your company and the challenges you are going through.
  • Post your advisory request. Tell us about what kind of advisors you’d like to bring into your venture’s journey.
  • We handpick the best fitting experts. We put the effort in fully understanding your business needs and invite world class experts to connect with you for advisory work.

Appoint your advisory board

We select the best experts for the advisory roles you need and invite them to connect with you.

  • Review them. Read through the advisor’ complete profile, their expertise, experience and references.
  • Get to know them. Have a discovery chat to know more about them and how they can bring value to your organisation.
  • Confirm appointment. Once comfortable, confirm you’d like to appoint them for the advisory work. You can appoint as many advisors as you see fit.

Leverage on your advisory team’s guidance & network

Once you have appointed your team of advisors it’s time to bringing them up to speed with your business for deeper insights.

  • Bring them up to speed. We ensure confidentiality and 0 conflict of interests so you can share key information for deeper insights.
  • Ask questions. Formulate the tough questions or problems you are facing and share them with your advisor.
  • Get introduced. Leverage on you advisor network and get introduced to investors, partners, potential clients etc.
